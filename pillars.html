Five Pillars of Islam

  1. Shahada (Faith) – Declaration of belief in Allah and His Messenger ﷺ.
  2. Salah (Prayer) – Performing the five daily prayers.
  3. Zakat (Charity) – Giving a portion of wealth to the needy.
  4. Sawm (Fasting in Ramadan) – Abstaining from food, drink, and desires during Ramadan.
  5. Hajj (Pilgrimage) – Journey to Makkah at least once if able.
