Daily Prayers (Salah)

Times & Steps

1. Fajr (Dawn Prayer)

Time: Before sunrise

Description: Performed at dawn. Two units (rak'ahs) of prayer to start the day in remembrance of Allah.

2. Dhuhr (Noon Prayer)

Time: After the sun passes its zenith

Description: Four rak'ahs performed in the early afternoon. A moment to pause and reflect during the day.

3. Asr (Afternoon Prayer)

Time: Late afternoon

Description: Four rak'ahs, reminding us to stay connected to Allah even during busy times.

4. Maghrib (Sunset Prayer)

Time: Just after sunset

Description: Three rak'ahs, marking the end of the day and showing gratitude for the blessings received.

5. Isha (Night Prayer)

Time: After twilight

Description: Four rak'ahs, a peaceful prayer before rest, reflecting on the day and seeking forgiveness.
